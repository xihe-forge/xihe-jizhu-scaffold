# Research Workflow

## Flow

Research feeds implementation through a simple chain:

`research -> MRD -> PRD -> technical design -> implementation`

## Outputs

- research notes: `docs/mrd/00-research.md`
- feature requirements: `docs/prd/`
- design and architecture: `docs/tech/` and `docs/design/`

## Exit Criteria

Do not start implementation unless the team can answer:

- Who is this for?
- What problem is being solved?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What is the smallest testable slice?
