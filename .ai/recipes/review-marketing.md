# Review Recipe: Marketing Strategy Audit

## When to Use

After creating marketing materials, positioning documents, or go-to-market plans.

## Required Tools

This review uses the scaffold's built-in marketing and go-to-market methodology. No external skill modules required.

## Review Checklist

### Stage 1: Positioning & Messaging

Apply these checks:

1. **Copywriting** (`copywriting`): Is messaging clear, benefit-driven, and jargon-free?
2. **Positioning** (`marketing-ideas`): Is the product positioned against alternatives (not just competitors)?
3. **Psychology** (`marketing-psychology`): Are persuasion principles applied ethically?
4. **Value proposition**: Is it clear what the product does, for whom, and why it's better?

### Stage 2: SEO & Content

1. **SEO audit** (`seo-audit`): Are target keywords identified? Is content structured for search?
2. **AI SEO** (`ai-seo`): Is content optimized for AI-powered search (LLM-friendly structure)?
3. **Site architecture** (`site-architecture`): Is information architecture logical?
4. **Content strategy** (`content`): Is there a content calendar or plan?

### Stage 3: Conversion & Growth

1. **Page CRO** (`page-cro`): Are landing pages optimized for conversion?
2. **Signup flow** (`signup-flow`): Is the registration/onboarding friction-free?
3. **Growth strategy** (`free-tool-strategy`, `referral-programs`): Is there a growth loop?
4. **Churn prevention** (`churn-prevention`): Are retention mechanisms planned?

### Stage 4: Go-to-Market

Apply go-to-market checks:

1. **Beachhead segment** (`beachhead-segment`): Is the initial target market narrow enough?
2. **ICP definition** (`icp-definition`): Is the Ideal Customer Profile specific and validated?
3. **GTM motion** (`gtm-motion`): Is the go-to-market motion clear (PLG, sales-led, community)?
4. **Launch strategy** (`launch-strategy`): Is there a phased launch plan?

### Stage 5: Competitive Battlecard

1. **Competitor battlecards** (`competitor-battlecards`): Are key competitors analyzed?
2. **Differentiation**: Are unique selling points defensible and verifiable?
3. **Pricing** (`pricing`): Is pricing strategy justified against alternatives?

## Pass / Fail Criteria

- **PASS**: All stages addressed, positioning clear, at least beachhead + ICP defined
- **FAIL**: No clear positioning, missing ICP, or no growth strategy
- On FAIL: Return with specific gaps. Marketing materials must not be published without review.

## Output

Record in `dev/review/REVIEW-MARKETING-{date}.md` with all stage results.
